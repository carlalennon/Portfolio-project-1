The meetup group for Dublin's animation community


We run monthly-ish meetups for animation staff to meet up, make new friends and catch up with old ones in an increasingly remote industry


The Event

What we do

Once a month, animation staff from all over come together to make connections in an increasingly remote world. Our event is usually hosted in JR Mahons on the quay. We meet to chat, relax, catch up and make new friends. There are comic jams and other paper games that encourage us to meet new people. We draw and play togther to remind us that it's not just about what we do, it's about who we do it with.

Why we matter

Attending an event like like DAM can benefit you both personally and professionally. We harbour an atmopshere of growth and learning. To get out there and introduce yourself can be hard. We provide a space for professionals to establish themselves among their peers with similar interests. Our friendly volunteers are there to support you, and be the first friend you make in a room full of strangers.
